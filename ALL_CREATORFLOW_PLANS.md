## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Do all plans have the same tools?
**A:** Yes! All plans have access to ALL core tools and AI bots. The difference is usage limits, number of accounts, AI quality, and advanced features.

### Q: What happens if I hit my limit on Free?
**A:** You'll see an upgrade prompt. You can continue using what you've created, but you'll need to upgrade to create more.

### Q: Can I use all tools on Free plan?
**A:** Yes! You can use all tools, just with limited usage (10 docs, 5 hashtag sets, 3 templates, 50 AI calls/month). However, you cannot make posts on the free plan.

### Q: What's the difference between AI feature levels?
**A:**
- **Basic (Free):** Rule-based, simple responses
- **Enhanced (Starter):** Better AI models, more accurate
- **Advanced (Growth):** Even better models, smarter responses
- **Premium (Pro):** Best AI models, most accurate
- **Maximum (Business/Agency):** Highest quality, fastest responses

### Q: Can I downgrade later?
**A:** Yes! You can change your plan at any time. Your content is always yours.

### Q: What happens to my data if I cancel?
**A:** Your data stays in your account. You can access it if you resubscribe. Free plan users keep their data forever.

### Q: Can I switch plans mid-month?
**A:** Yes! Plan changes take effect immediately. You'll be charged/prorated accordingly.

### Q: Do I need to enter payment info for Free plan?
**A:** No! Free plan requires no payment information.

### Q: What payment methods do you accept?
**A:** All major credit cards, debit cards, and Apple Pay/Google Pay via Stripe.
