## âš ï¸ Important Notes

### Reserve Funds:
- Stripe may hold some funds in reserve (especially for new accounts)
- Reserve is usually 5-10% of revenue
- Reserve is released after 90 days of good payment history

### Minimum Payout:
- Usually $1 minimum to withdraw
- No maximum limit

### Payout Speed:
- **Standard**: 2-7 business days (free)
- **Instant**: Same day (1% fee, up to $5,000)
