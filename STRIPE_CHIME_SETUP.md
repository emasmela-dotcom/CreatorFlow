# üè¶ Stripe + Chime Setup - Manual Payouts Guide

## Step 1: Connect Your Chime Account to Stripe

1. **In Stripe Dashboard** ‚Üí Look at the left sidebar
2. **Find and click** "Settings" (usually near the bottom)
3. **Click "Bank accounts and scheduling"** (under Business settings)
4. **Or go directly to**: https://dashboard.stripe.com/settings/payouts

### Add Your Chime Account:

5. **Click "Add bank account"** or **"Add external account"** button
6. **You'll see a form to fill in:**
   - **Account holder name**: Enter exactly as it appears on your Chime account
   - **Account type**: Select "Checking" (unless Chime account is savings)
   - **Routing number**: Enter your Chime routing number
   - **Account number**: Enter your Chime account number

7. **Click "Add bank account"** or **"Save"**

8. **Stripe will verify** (may take 1-2 days):
   - They'll make 2 small test deposits (usually $0.01-$0.99 each)
   - You'll need to confirm these amounts in Stripe

---

## Step 2: Set Up Manual Payouts

### After your Chime account is connected and verified:

1. **Still in Payout settings** (Settings ‚Üí Bank accounts and scheduling)

2. **Find "Payout schedule"** section

3. **Look for the schedule dropdown** - it might say:
   - "Automatic daily"
   - "Automatic weekly" 
   - "Automatic monthly"
   - Or show a calendar

4. **Click on the schedule** or **"Edit"** button next to it

5. **Change to "Manual"**:
   - Select "Manual" from the dropdown
   - Or toggle "Manual payouts" to ON
   - Look for "Manual" option in the scheduling options

6. **Save the changes**

---

## Step 3: How Manual Payouts Work

**Once set to Manual:**

- ‚úÖ Funds will accumulate in your Stripe balance
- ‚úÖ No automatic transfers happen
- ‚úÖ You control when to pay out

**When you're ready to transfer funds:**

1. Go to Stripe Dashboard ‚Üí **"Payouts"** (left sidebar)
2. You'll see your **"Available balance"**
3. Click **"Pay out"** button
4. Choose which bank account (your Chime account)
5. Click **"Pay out"** to confirm
6. Funds will transfer (usually 2-7 business days)

---

## Step 4: Verify Chime Account (After Test Deposits)

**Stripe will make 2 small test deposits** to verify your account:

1. **Wait 1-2 business days** for test deposits to appear in Chime
2. **Check your Chime transaction history** for 2 small deposits
3. **Note the exact amounts** (e.g., $0.32 and $0.15)
4. **Go back to Stripe** ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Bank accounts
5. **Click "Verify"** or find your Chime account and click it
6. **Enter the 2 test deposit amounts** in the form
7. **Click "Verify"** or **"Confirm"**

**Once verified**, you can:
- Set payout schedule to Manual
- Start receiving payouts to Chime

## üÜò Troubleshooting

**Can't find payout settings?**
- Try: Settings ‚Üí Payment methods ‚Üí Bank accounts
- Or: https://dashboard.stripe.com/settings/payouts

**Don't see "Manual" option?**
- Some accounts need to be verified first
- Contact Stripe support if Manual option isn't available
- You can also use "Monthly" schedule as an alternative

**Test deposits not showing in Chime?**
- Wait up to 3 business days
- Check Chime app transaction history
- Verify you entered the correct account numbers

**Need help finding your Chime routing/account numbers?**
- Open Chime app ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Account Info
- Or Chime website ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Account Details
