## Why This Works

1. **Starter is genuinely minimal** - Just organization, no automation
2. **Each tier adds clear value** - Not just more of the same
3. **Progressive feature unlocks** - AI bots unlock at Growth, full suite at Pro
4. **Clear upgrade path** - Each step feels worth it
5. **Competitive pricing** - $9 entry point beats most competitors
6. **Value perception** - Higher tiers feel premium, not just "more posts"
