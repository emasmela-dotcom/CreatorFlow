STRIPE WEBHOOK SETUP - STEP BY STEP
====================================

Step 1: Open Stripe Dashboard
- Go to: https://dashboard.stripe.com
- Make sure you're in LIVE MODE (toggle top-right)
- Click "Developers" in left sidebar
- Click "Webhooks" in submenu

Step 2: Add Endpoint
- Click "+ Add endpoint" button (top-right)
- Paste URL: https://creatorflow-iota.vercel.app/api/stripe/webhook

Step 3: Select Events
Check these 4 boxes:
☐ checkout.session.completed
☐ customer.subscription.created
☐ customer.subscription.updated
☐ invoice.payment_failed

Step 4: Save
- Click "Add endpoint"

Step 5: Copy Secret
- Click on the new webhook
- Click "Reveal" next to "Signing secret"
- Copy the whsec_... value

Step 6: Verify in Vercel
- Check if STRIPE_WEBHOOK_SECRET matches
- Update if needed
