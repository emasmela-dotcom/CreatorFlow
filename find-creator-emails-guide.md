# How to Find Creator Emails

## Method 1: Direct Contact Info (Most Reliable)

### Check Creator's Social Media:
1. **Instagram Bio** - Many creators list "Email: [email]" or "Contact: [email]"
2. **Link in Bio** - Click through to their website/landing page
3. **YouTube About Page** - Check "For business inquiries" section
4. **TikTok Bio** - Some list email addresses

### Check Creator's Website:
- Most creators have a website/blog
- Look for "Contact" or "Work With Me" page
- Check footer for email address
- Look for "Press Kit" or "Media Kit" page

## Method 2: Email Finding Tools

### Free Tools:
1. **Hunter.io** - Find emails by domain
   - If creator has website, enter domain
   - Shows email patterns and verified emails
   - Free tier: 25 searches/month

2. **Email Finder Extensions:**
   - **Lusha** (Chrome extension)
   - **RocketReach** (Chrome extension)
   - **Snov.io** (Chrome extension)
   - Install extension, visit creator's LinkedIn/profile, click extension

3. **Google Search:**
   - Search: `"[creator name]" email contact`
   - Search: `"[creator name]" "@gmail.com" OR "@outlook.com"`
   - Search: `site:[creatorwebsite.com] contact email`

### Paid Tools (More Accurate):
1. **Apollo.io** - $49/month, finds emails + LinkedIn
2. **ZoomInfo** - Enterprise level
3. **Clearbit** - API for finding emails

## Method 3: Social Media Direct Messages

### If you can't find email:
1. **Instagram DM** - Send professional message
2. **Twitter DM** - If they have DMs open
3. **LinkedIn Message** - Professional outreach
4. **TikTok Comments** - Less professional, but can work

## Method 4: Creator Platforms

### Platforms that list creator contact info:
1. **Creator.co** - Creator marketplace
2. **AspireIQ** - Brand-creator platform
3. **Upfluence** - Influencer database
4. **Grin** - Creator marketing platform
5. **Tagger** - Influencer discovery

## Method 5: Manual Research Process

### Step-by-step:
1. **Find creators** on Instagram/TikTok/YouTube (1K-100K followers)
2. **Check their bio** for website/email
3. **Visit their website** → Look for contact page
4. **Check YouTube About tab** → Business email
5. **Search Google** → "[name] email" or "[name] contact"
6. **Use Hunter.io** → Enter their website domain
7. **Check LinkedIn** → Some creators list email
8. **Try Instagram DM** → If no email found

## Target Creator Criteria for CreatorFlow:

### Ideal Creators:
- **Follower count:** 1,000 - 100,000 (micro to mid-tier)
- **Platforms:** Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Twitter
- **Niche:** Any (fitness, tech, lifestyle, business, etc.)
- **Engagement:** Active, posting regularly
- **Location:** US/English-speaking preferred

### Where to Find Them:
1. **Instagram Explore** - Search hashtags like #contentcreator #creator
2. **TikTok Discover** - Search "content creator" or specific niches
3. **YouTube Search** - "how to grow on [platform]" videos
4. **Reddit** - r/ContentCreators, r/YouTubers, r/TikTokMarketing
5. **Twitter** - Search #CreatorEconomy, #ContentCreator

## Email Format Patterns to Try:

If you know creator's name and website:
- firstname@website.com
- hello@website.com
- contact@website.com
- name@website.com
- info@website.com

Use Hunter.io to verify which pattern works.

## Ethical Considerations:

✅ **DO:**
- Only email creators who publicly list their email
- Personalize each email
- Offer clear value (free membership)
- Make it easy to opt-out

❌ **DON'T:**
- Scrape emails without permission
- Send spam
- Buy email lists
- Email without clear value proposition
